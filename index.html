At BrightCore Electrical, we are not just another electrical contractor. We are a passionate team of professionals dedicated to installing exceptional electrical systems and services while providing an unmatched experience for our clients.

What we do

COMMERCIAL

  • Electrical consulting and project planning for new constructions.
  • Primary and secondary duct bank, advanced electrical Panel, transformer, switchgear, hydro vault installation and servicing.
  • Setup of advanced, compliant electrical systems.
  • Compliance testing and certification.
  • Emergency troubleshooting and repair, available 24/7.
  • Regular system audits for safety and performance.
  • Custom commercial lighting design and setup.
  • Implementation of sustainable, energy-efficient solutions.
  • Fire Alarm and Security.
